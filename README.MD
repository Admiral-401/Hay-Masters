

A modern, responsive website for **Hays Business**, a Kenyan agribusiness specializing in high-quality Boma Rhodes hay. This site showcases the business, its services, core values, testimonials, and provides a newsletter subscription form to stay connected with clients and partners.

## About

Hays Business is a Kenyan-based agribusiness founded by **Mr. John Moraya Kareithi** alongside his partners and colleagues. The company is committed to supplying the Kenyan market with **high-quality Boma Rhodes hay** to support livestock health and sustainable farming.  

The website highlights:

- Business overview and vision  
- Mission and core values  
- Customer testimonials  
- Newsletter subscription  
- Contact and call-to-action button (WhatsApp)  

---

## Features

- **Responsive Design**: Works seamlessly on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices  
- **Interactive Slideshow**: Showcases hay products or images  
- **WhatsApp Floating Button**: Direct contact link with pulse animation  
- **Newsletter Subscription Form**: Allows users to subscribe to updates  
- **Clean Layout**: Modern design with sections for About, Vision, Mission, Values, Testimonials  
- **Animations**: Smooth hover and button effects  

---

## Technologies Used

- **HTML5** – Semantic structure  
- **CSS3** – Styling, responsive layout, animations  
- **Flexbox** – Layout for sections, slideshow, testimonials  
- **Optional JavaScript** – For slideshow and interactive elements
